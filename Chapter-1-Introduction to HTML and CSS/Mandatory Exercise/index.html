Strawberry crepe cake

A perfect treat for any occasion to enjoy with friends, family or even by yourself.
The rich creamy texture and the sweet taste makes everyone's day better as the taste melts into your mouth as how cotton candy melts,
the rich taste of strawberry is an additional remark of the tasty pastry.

Ingredients

  • Strawberry
  • Whipped cream
  • 200g cake flour
  • 50g sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 400g milk
  • 70g melted unsalted butter
  • A pinch of salt
  • 100g almond milk

Method

  1. Take a bowl and add 4 eggs, a pinch of salt and 50g sugar and whisk it.
  2. Now take a strainer and put 200g cake flour in it to get the mixture without lumps.
  3. Add 100g almond milk to the cake mixture and start whisking it till the you get a smooth texture.
  4. Add 400g milk, whisk it and then add 70g melted unsalted butter and start whisking.
  5. Pour the mixture to jar and keep it in room temperature for 30 minutes.
  6. Take a pan and pour cooking oil and spread it evenly over the pan till dried.
  7. Take the cake mixture and take a scoop of mixture and pour it in pan and spread it out.
  8. Flip the mixture after a minute or 2, after both sides are nicely cooked, take the crepe out.
  9. Do the same stuff over till you reach the desired amount of crepes.
  10. Add whipping cream on the crepe and add sliced strawbberies and layer it by adding another crepe on top.
  11. Do the same thing over till the end and spread out the whipping cream on top.
  12. Keep the cake in fridge to harden for a while. and decorate the cake by adding full sized strawberries as topping and pour powdered sugar on top.
