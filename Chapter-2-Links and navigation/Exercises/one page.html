overview

Rekhachithramis a mystery alternative historical film directed by jofin.T.Chacko,the story is set around when vivek Gopinath, a suspended cop rejoins the force after a gambling scandal is tasked with solving a 40 year old murder case of a faceless victim.

characters

  • Vivek Gopinath a cop suspend from the force for gambling scandal, trying to seek redemption thrrough cracking a 40 year old murder mystery.
  • Rekha Pathrose a young aspiring actress, who is a big fan of Mammootty.later identified as the faceless victim.
  • Mammotty a huge superstar who is there for his shoot of the film 'Kathodu Kathoram'.
  • Vincent a business man , who is highly suspected and investigated as one of the culprit of the murder case.
  • Rajendran one of the culprits who took part in the burial of the body and who took his own life due to guilt and admitted the crime through live.
  • Francis Thadathil another culprit whose name was mentioned by Rajendran during his live confession.
  • Gopinath the father of CI vivek Gopinath.
  • Chandrappan the production controller of the film 'Kathodu Kathoram'.
  • Pushpam later known as alice is the wife of vincent and the mastermind behind the crime.
  • DYSP Mohandas superior of Vivek.
  • Sareena is Vivek's partner and a journalist.
  • Sister Stephy a nun who was there during thr shooting of the film 'Kathodu Kathoram'and the who let rekha stay at her place.
  • Dr.Sajan a forensic docter.
  • Asha Pathrose the sister of Rekha Pathrose.
